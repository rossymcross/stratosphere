THINGS TO KNOW:
You must be 15 years or older to participate in axe throwing. All throwers must present a valid ID at the time of check-in to prove that you are at least 15 or older.
All throwers must wear close-toed shoes.
Only paid throwers will be allowed inside the axe throwing arena.
All participants must sign a liability waiver before they begin. All minors (15+) must have a parent or legal guardian sign a liability waiver for them. Click here to sign it now.
Food & drinks will be available for purchase at the lanes.
All throwers will be limited to 2 alcoholic beverages during their 1-hour reservation.
If you arrive intoxicated, you will not be allowed to participate.
Before you begin, one of our certified Axe Coaches will provide your group with rules and safety instructions.
No more than 6 people per lane. Groups larger than 6 will have to reserve multiple lanes.
