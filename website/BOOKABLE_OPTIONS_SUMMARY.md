# Stratosphere Social - Bookable Options Summary

This document summarizes all bookable attractions, party packages, and events available at Stratosphere Social, based on the audit of the existing website content.

## 1. Attractions (Walk-in & Reservations)

### Axe Throwing
*   **Format**: Lane reservations for up to 6 people.
*   **Age Restriction**: 15+ (ID required).
*   **Pricing (Per Person)**:
    *   **30 Minutes**:
        *   Tue/Wed: $15
        *   Thu: $7.50 (50% off)
        *   Fri-Sun: $20
    *   **1 Hour**:
        *   Tue/Wed: $20
        *   Thu: $10 (50% off)
        *   Fri-Sun: $28
        *   **Axes & Ales (Mon 6pm-9pm)**: $10 (includes beer discounts)
    *   **2 Hours**:
        *   Tue/Wed: $34
        *   Thu: $17 (50% off)
        *   Fri-Sun: $50
*   **Details**: Interactive projected targets. Includes safety briefing and coach. Closed-toed shoes required.

### Bowling
*   **Format**: Lane rentals (up to 6 people per lane).
*   **Policy**: First come, first served (unless booking a party package).
*   **Pricing (Per Lane/Hour)**:
    *   **Mon, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun**: $40 + tax
    *   **Tue, Wed**: $20 + tax (50% off special)
*   **Extras**: Shoe Rentals: $5.00 per pair.

### Arcade
*   **Format**: Card-operated system.
*   **Booking**: No specific booking; cards purchased/loaded at kiosk or counter.
*   **Details**: Points earned for free games; E-Tickets for prizes.

### XD Theater
*   **Format**: 7D Multi-sensory ride (8-10 min movies).
*   **Booking**: Typically included in packages or pay-per-ride (specific single ride price not listed in audit, but included in "Level Up" and "Supercharged" packages).

## 2. Party Packages (10+ Guests)

*Note: All parties require a minimum of 10 guests. A 20% service charge is added to all packages.*

### Bowling & Arcade Parties
*   **Press Play Game Zone**
    *   **Price**: $180 (Mon-Sun)
    *   **Includes**: 60-min arcade card, 1 one-topping pizza, soda pitchers, party supplies.
    *   **Extra Guest**: $18 each.
*   **Level Up Game Zone**
    *   **Price**: $329 (Mon-Thu) / $340 (Fri-Sun)
    *   **Includes**: 60-min arcade card, 2 games VR/XD Theater, 2 pizzas, unlimited soda, host.
    *   **Extra Guest**: $25 each.
*   **Supercharged Game Zone**
    *   **Price**: $440 (Mon-Thu) / $465 (Fri-Sun)
    *   **Includes**: 120-min arcade card, unlimited VR/XD Theater, 2 pizzas, unlimited soda, host.
    *   **Extra Guest**: $35 each.

### Karaoke Parties
*   **Standard Karaoke Party**
    *   **Price**: $250 (Mon-Thu) / $300 (Fri-Sun)
    *   **Capacity**: Up to 15 people.
    *   **Includes**: 2 hours private room, 30-min arcade card per person, dedicated server.
*   **Themed Karaoke Party** (Wicked, Taylor Swift, K-Pop)
    *   **Price**: $425 (Mon-Thu) / $475 (Fri-Sun)
    *   **Capacity**: Up to 10 people (Extra guest $25).
    *   **Includes**: 2 hours private room, themed decor/music, 30-min arcade card, 2 pizzas, unlimited soda.

### Action Parties
*   **Nerf War Party**
    *   **Price**: $489 (Mon-Thu) / $515 (Fri-Sun)
    *   **Includes**: 1 hour Nerf War, 60-min arcade card, 250 tickets, 2 pizzas, unlimited soda, host.
    *   **Extra Guest**: $40 each.
*   **Axe & Bowl Combo**
    *   **Price**: $50 per person (Min 10 people).
    *   **Includes**: 1 hour axe throwing, 1 hour bowling (shoes included), 30-min arcade card, 1 VR/XD game.

## 3. Special Events & Entertainment

### Dueling Pianos
*   **VIP 8-Top Table**: $160 (Reserved seating for 8).
*   **VIP 4-Top Table**: $80 (Reserved seating for 4).
*   **General Admission**: $15/person (First come, first served community seating).

### Trivia Night
*   **Schedule**: Thursdays 7pm-9pm.
*   **Cost**: Free to play.
*   **Format**: Teams up to 8 people. First come, first served seating.

### New Year's Eve (Seasonal Example)
*   **DD/Under 21 Ticket**: $40 (Advance) / $55 (Door).
*   **Standard Ticket (21+)**: $60 (Advance) / $75 (Door).
*   **Includes**: Unlimited attractions (Bowl, Axe, Arcade, VR/XD), food/drink specials.
