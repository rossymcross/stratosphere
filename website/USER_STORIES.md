Prioritized User Personas (by Business Value)
1. The "Corporate Assistant" (Event Planner) - HIGHEST VALUE

Revenue Impact: $$$$ (Largest group sizes 50-200+, premium packages, catering, private rooms, recurring business)
Average Booking Value: Typically $2,000-$10,000+
Strategic Value: Brand visibility, referrals, repeat annual events

2. The "Busy Parent" (Planner) - HIGH VALUE

Revenue Impact: $$$ (Party packages for 10-30 kids, food/beverage, repeat customers)
Average Booking Value: $400-$1,500
Strategic Value: Word-of-mouth marketing, creates loyal family customers

3. The "Social Butterfly" (Young Adult / 21+) - MEDIUM-HIGH VALUE

Revenue Impact: $$ (Groups of 4-12, high beverage margins, prime-time bookings)
Average Booking Value: $200-$600 per visit
Strategic Value: Social media exposure, repeat visits, brings energy/atmosphere

4. The "Foodie" (Diner) - MEDIUM VALUE

Revenue Impact: $$ (Food/beverage only, smaller party sizes 2-6)
Average Booking Value: $50-$150 per visit
Strategic Value: Fills capacity during off-peak, brings non-traditional guests

5. The "Local Regular" (Community Member) - LOWER DIRECT VALUE

Revenue Impact: $ (Utility visits, job applications, donation requests)
Average Booking Value: Varies or $0
